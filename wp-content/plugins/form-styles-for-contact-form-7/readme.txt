Add stunning styles for forms that created by contact form 7. Impress your visitors with beautiful, professionally designed contact form themes

== Description ==

Contact form 7 is a great contact form plugin. However, the generic styling doesn't make your site look well in the eyes of your visitors. 

Contact forms should look professional since it partly represent your image. With Contact form 7 style plugin, you will get your contact page designed for free.

With a decent collection of contact form themes, you can switch between styles easily to make your contact page fit to your current theme. 

No matter what theme you are using for your site, you can easily find a suitable style for your contact page. 

This plugin is proudly presented by the team at core37.com

== Installation ==

1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
